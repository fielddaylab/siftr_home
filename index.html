What Is Siftr?
Siftr extends learning beyond your computer, books, classroom walls - preparing people of all ages to learn in the wild. A tool for teachers and curious people. Siftr supports you as you explore the world.

Siftr helps you focus, collect, visualize, discuss
Icon
Ready, set, focus:
Let's face it, the world is too much to take in all at once. Siftr helps you focus before you set out to explore.
Icon
Collect:
Siftr collects all your discoveries into one spot where everyone you want can see and add to them. It lets you collaborate with others on your projects.
Icon
See your discoveries:
Siftr visualizes everything you collect - the photos and the places you found them. In the future, Siftr will create charts, graphs, overlays, and more.
Icon
Discuss:
Siftr smoothly faciilitates discussions whether you are in a classroom setting, sitting over tea with a friend, or a world away. It can privately engage specific communitites, like a middle school history class or a group of neighbors.
